Privacy

Plain language. No surprises.

What this site collects

Earth Office does not have user accounts, does not run its own analytics, and does not track you across the web. There are no forms that collect personal information, no cookies set by this site directly, and no databases storing anything about visitors.

Like most websites, this one is served through a web host and a CDN (Cloudflare). Standard server logs — IP addresses, request timestamps, browser types — are generated automatically as part of how the internet works. These logs exist at the infrastructure level and are not used for profiling or marketing.

Advertising

This site displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google is a third party — it has its own systems, its own cookies, and its own privacy practices, which are separate from anything this site does.

Google may use cookies and data about your browsing activity to show you ads that are relevant to your interests. This is standard behavior for AdSense. You can read more about how Google handles data in their own privacy policy:

policies.google.com/privacy

You can also manage your ad personalization settings through Google's ad settings page, or opt out of interest-based advertising through the Network Advertising Initiative.

Third-party tools and links

Some projects hosted here may embed or link to external services — APIs, data sources, or interactive tools. Each of those services has its own privacy practices. This policy only covers earthoffice.net itself.
